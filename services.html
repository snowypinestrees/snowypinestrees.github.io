Services

Shake em' up!

Once you pick out your tree we will shake free any stray pine needles to ensure a clean transition from our lot to your home. The tree won't be harmed, and your floors will thank us!

Protection and stands

We care that your tree stays happy and healthy, that’s why we give the trunk a fresh cut, before installing leak-proof water stands and offer the choice of netting or bagging for a secure and protected journey home.

We'll carry it, and tie it!

Our employees at Snowy Pines are here to help you from start to finish, once your tree is ready to go, we will take it to your car and tie it up for you. All you have to worry about is picking out the perfect tree!

Have your tree flocked!

Elevate your holiday ambiance with our tree flocking service, providing a picturesque snowy finish that captures the essence of a classic winter landscape right in your living room.

We deliver!

Simplify your holiday preparations with our delivery services! Whether tying the tree to your car is not your preference or you're unable to make it to the lot, we can bring the festive cheer straight to your doorstep. Ask us about delivery options!
